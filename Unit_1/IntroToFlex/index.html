Flex Heading

Accomplish

Horizontal or Vertical arrangement of elements.

Space distribution of elements.

Quick Notes

It is one-dimensional. This will either effect a row or column, but not both at the same time.

Useful for layout on nav bars, headers, img galleries, etc.

Will place elements that are direct children, but doesn't effect further children.

Flex Container

The element with the 'display:flex' style. This style applies to the container and its children.

Justifying Content

Position content along the axis of the flex container.

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. space-between
  5. space-around
  6. space-evenly

Align Items

Similar to Justify Content, but works along the cross-axis.

  1. flex-start
  2. flex-end
  3. center
  4. stretch
  5. baseline
